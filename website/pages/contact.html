Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?
Most customers are up and running within 2-4 weeks. Our team handles the entire setup process, including IoT integration and model training.
What types of equipment can you monitor?
AISPARK supports pumps, compressors, motors, turbines, ovens, presses, conveyors, and virtually any industrial equipment with sensors or IoT connectivity.
Do you integrate with existing systems?
Yes! We integrate with most SCADA systems, PLCs, and existing IoT platforms. Our universal protocols support MQTT, OPC-UA, Modbus, and more.
What's included in the pricing?
All plans include the platform, ML models, mobile apps, API access, and support. Enterprise plans add custom integrations and dedicated support.
How accurate is the anomaly detection?
Our ensemble AI models achieve 97.3% accuracy with less than 2% false positives. Accuracy improves over time as models learn your specific equipment patterns.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial with full functionality. No credit card required. We'll help you set up monitoring for up to 5 machines.
